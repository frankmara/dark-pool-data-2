### [Upcoming Changes to Bid and Ask Sizes in Stocks Quotes](https://massive.com/blog/change-stocks-quotes-round-lots-to-shares)

Effective Nov 3, 2025, bid\_size/ask\_size will be reported in shares (not round lots) across Stocks Quotes REST API, WebSocket, and Flat Files, per SEC MDI rules. The rule is forward-looking and we’ll also backfill history for consistency. Most users need no changes.
